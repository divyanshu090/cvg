Budget Shopping at SSS

Visiting the Sunday Soul Sante on a tight budget? We have something for everyone! Check out these categories and vendors at our market and find something for yourself even on a tight budget!

Stationary

Find stickers, bejewelled stationery, handmade bookmarks, badges and more at our stationery vendors. You can also find student entrepreneurs at our Heart Of Sante corner, selling some unique stationery and art pieces on a budget!

Snacks

Chips, healthy popped snacks, pickles, baked granola bars, candies, dried fruits, jerkies - you name it! Find our snack vendors within the general shopping area and grab a few snacks to munch on as you explore, or just to take home! Most of them might even give you a sample to try!

Pottery

Create your own pottery works of art with Lokesh, our designated potter for the event. You can also buy small pieces of pottery from him and even around the event at a very reasonable price!

Body Art

Get your face painted, arm tattooed, or just a simple dread on your head! Find our face and body artists in the event to give you a unique look for the event!

Books

Buy preloved and new books from our book vendors at the Sante! You can even bring preloved books from your own collection and do a little book exchange (at the discretion of the vendor)!

Plants

What better way to create a memory than to buy a plant to immortalise your experience? Get a succulent for your desk, a palm tree for your room or a pretty outdoor plant to add to your home garden. You’ll find all these options at the Indo-American Hybrid Seeds stall right at the entrance to the Sante.
