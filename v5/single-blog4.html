Fun for All: What to do when you’re not shopping at SSS

The Sunday Soul Sante is a shopper’s paradise! You can find the most unique arts and crafts, apparel and jewellery, collectibles, home decor and almost everything under the sun. But what to do if you’re not a shopaholic, or visiting on a tight budget? Well, we’ve got something for everyone!

Selfie Corner and Photo Opportunities

Right at the entrance to the Sunday Soul Sante, you’ll find a selfie corner with several props that you can use to take your Instagram-worthy selfies to post on the day of the event. But that’s not all! As you enter, you’ll find many spots around the market where you can capture beautiful memories of the day. And as it starts to get dark, you’ll see lights across the event, with themed decor and fairy lights at the stalls! Each vendor decorates their own stall too, so don’t forget to capture a moment at your favourite vendors’ stalls as well!

Tattoo Art, Face Painters and More

As you go around the Sante, you’ll find our talented artists among the stalls making tattoos, face painting, or even creating dreads! Find what speaks to you and support an artist while your friends and family shop!

Tarot Reading

Want to delve into the mystical world and see what the future holds? There is always a tarot reader that you can head to for a reading. Our readers are personally vetted by our team and are authentic and professional.

Artists

Sunday Soul Sante is nothing less than a party! Head to the mainstage area to find the event’s performers and start the party! Our event has been graced by India’s most loved performers like Thaikkudam Bridge, Raghu Dixit, Ayushman Khurrana, Indus Creed, and more in the past and we bring new artists each time!

Beer, Cocktails, and Snacks

And what is a party without food and drinks? Grab a beer or a cocktail from our bars near the mainstage or some food and snacks from the food court and get ready for an unforgettable evening! Our bars and food courts are open all day, so don’t hesitate to start your party early!

Pet Area

Visiting with a pet? We are a fully pet friendly event, and our entire market is accessible to your fur babies! We also have pet friendly and pet exclusive vendors so you can grab a snack for your pet friends on the go! Don’t have a pet? Adopt one from the Lets Live Together stall! Just make sure to carry a poop scoop and disposables!

Kids Area

If you’re visiting with kids, we’ve got something in store for them too! Visit our kids area with rides, games and other fun activities to keep your kids entertained for hours while you shop! We also have many vendors within the stalls selling exclusive apparel, snacks, skincare, and merchandise for kids so keep an eye out for them too!

Samplers

Want to try it before you buy it? Many of our vendors also keep samplers of their products for you to get a sense of what you’re buying and get the best bang for your buck. But please be respectful and only take a sample if you are actually interested in the product or want to sample the brand for future purchases.
