About Us

India's Largest Flea Market Celebrating Contemporary Arts and Crafts

Sunday Soul Sante, India's largest flea market situated in Bangalore since 2010, is a vibrant celebration of contemporary arts and crafts.

It showcases an extensive collection of handmade products ranging from clothing and accessories to jewelry and home decor.

Beyond shopping, the event offers live music, performances, and delectable food options, making it an ideal outing for friends and family.

This market is a unique opportunity to break away from the ordinary and be inspired by the inventive ideas of talented artists.

From clothing to accessories, jewelry to paintings, visitors can hunt for exceptional treasures at remarkable prices.

The event's lively atmosphere is further enriched by live shows and performances, ensuring a captivating experience for all the senses.

Sunday Soul Sante is a must-visit for those seeking a blend of creativity, entertainment, and culinary delights – an extraordinary escapade that shouldn't be missed.
