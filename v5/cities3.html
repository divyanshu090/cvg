Pune

An extraordinary collaboration with Vh1 Supersonic and Viacom18 brought a wave of music and shopping to the Sunday Soul Sante flea market at Vh1 Supersonic in Pune, February 2023. Witnessing the triumphant return of Vh1 Supersonic after three years, a three day star-studded lineup featuring global sensations like Anne-Marie and CKay, alongside India's finest musicians including Divine, Prateek Kuhad, OAFF & Savera.
With the energetic beats of Bob Moses, Camelphat, and DJ Bill Brewster, the atmosphere was electrifying throughout the evening. Meanwhile, our vibrant flea market attracted Pune's dynamic community, indulging in a delightful shopping experience while grooving to incredible tunes. Crowd strolled through the flea market to shop unique and quirky accessories, streatwear, decor, apparel, skincare and so much more! Proudly collaborating with Vh1 Supersonic and Viacom18, we transformed Mahalakshmi Lawns into a hub of music, and fashion.
