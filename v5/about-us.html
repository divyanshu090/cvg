About Us

India's Largest Flea Market Celebrating Contemporary Arts and Crafts

Sunday Soul Sante, since 2010, is India's largest flea market based out of Bangalore. It is a celebration of contemporary arts and crafts, featuring a wide variety of handmade products from talented artists and designers. Visitors can find everything from clothing and accessories to jewellery and home decor. In addition to shopping, Sunday Soul Sante also offers a variety of other activities, such as live music, food, and performances. It is a great place to spend a day with family and friends and to discover unique and handmade treasures.




Our Founders

Asha Rao

Meet Asha Rao, the founder of Sunday Soul Sante.

Asha's unwavering passion for fabric and arts has driven her to constantly regenerate her ideas, even in the face of setbacks.

Her reward is the success of Soul Sante. Asha Rao's foresight led to the birth of Sunday Soul Sante, a 15-year-old story rooted in the belief that aspiring artists need a platform to succeed.

Vishal Rao

Vishal Rao, an integral part of Sunday Soul Sante from its inception, brings unparalleled expertise in execution and reasoning.

With his vibrant personality and dedication, he ensures smooth operations of this grand event.

Vishal is our go-to person for everything, embodying a happy-go-lucky, people-oriented approach. In his free time, he enjoys playing golf, showcasing his patience and commitment to excellence.

Harish Rao

Harish Rao, with over 15 years of experience in Sales & Marketing and Business Development, is a driving force behind the growth of brands.

As the driving force behind Sunday Soul Sante's marketing, sponsorships, and alliances, Harish has established partnerships with renowned brands such as United Breweries, Times Of India, TVS, and Amazon.

He focuses on creating long-term partnerships with each sponsor while coordinating promotional campaigns with multiple brands. He believes that the journey is as important as the destination in both life and business.

Karan Rao

Karan Rao, a film producer with over five years of experience, brings essential impetus to the Sunday Soul Sante team.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for providing unique festival experiences, Karan handles the creative conceptualization, logistical planning, and execution for all Funday Events properties.

He is a staunch supporter of the Indian independent music scene, ensuring its prominence at Sunday Soul Sante. Karan's enthusiasm for fitness and sports translates into a healthy and energetic atmosphere within the entire SSS team.
