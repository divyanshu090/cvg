The Beginner’s Guide to SSS

First timer at the Sunday Soul Sante? Here’s everything you need to know and look out for!

Selfie Corner

Before you enter our massive event; remember to take a moment and capture a selfie with your gang at our selfie corner. It’s usually right beside the entrance and is a sight to behold! And don’t worry if you miss it in the beginning, you can always take a picture before you leave, as the selfie corner is even more beautiful when lit up in the evening!

Plants

More often than not, you will spot a large display of plants right in the beginning of the Sante when you enter the market. They’re usually in the first couple of stalls and have succulents and small plants starting at Rs.50! Definitely pick one (or ten!) up when you’re in there!

Apparel and Jewellery

From sustainable fashion to handmade jewellery - the variety of apparel and jewellery at the Sunday Soul Sante is to look out for! Find handcrafted apparel and jewellery, spanning a number of crafts from crochet to macrame, handblock, ikat and more. Get Indianwear, westernwear, indowestern fashion, kidswear, handloom sarees and handcrafted lehengas, stolls, scarves, menswear, athleisure, loungewear, swimwear, streetwear and the list goes on. So regardless of what you’re looking for, you’ll definitely be spoilt for choice in our apparel and jewellery stalls!

Pets Area

Got a fur baby? No problem! Bring them along and share your Soul Sante experience with them! We have plenty of stalls to cater to your pet’s needs. Just make sure to carry their poop sccop, disposables etc, to ensure cleanliness at the venue!

Kids Area

At Sunday Soul Sante, we have something for everyone - even kids! Your kids can enjoy the games, activities and rides at the kids’ area, while you enjoy shopping! Also find curated kids’ brands, selling kids clothes, accessories, snacks, toys and more!

Food Court, Snacks and Confectionery

Get ready to grab some scrumptious food at the Sunday Soul Sante! Experience some of the best brands, home chefs and bakers, putting their best foot forward. Enjoy flavourful biryanis, pizzas, mouthwatering rolls and kebabs, and an amalgamation of foods from across the city, country, and the world! And plenty of snacks, too, in case you want to get smaller portions through the day, or with your beer. And it doesn’t end there - get loads of confectionery and dessert options sprinkled throughout the market.

Services

Besides shopping stalls, we also have a curation of service stalls at the market. Unsure about the future? Get a tarot reading! Want a makeover? Get face paints, hair extensions and dreads, or even a temporary tattoo! Want to learn something new? Try the pottery store or a quick fluid art workshop! Plenty of ways to spend your day at our markets, that go beyond just shopping!

Artists and Performances

We’ve had the pleasure of hosting some of India’s best artists over the past few events that have attracted crowds from all over the country. We’ve had Ayushman Khurrana, Raghu Dixit, Thaikkudam Bridge, Indus Creed, Swarathma, Shanka Tribe, The Pineapple Express, and many more extremely talented artists performing some of their most legendary works at the Sunday Soul Sante! Look out for these performances in the mainstage area!

Beverages

Sunday Soul Sante is an amalgamation of fun, flea, food, artists, crafts, pets and more. It is a giant party that you can attend with your friends, colleagues, families, date or even just your pets! And our alcohol and beverage partners make this party even more memorable, with their craft mocktails, cocktails, beers and other beverages. Find them near the stage area and around the market, and grab a drink!
