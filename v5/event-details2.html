BOHEMIAN RHAPSODY

  Home   ‚ÅÑ   Bohemian Rhapsody

Experience the enchanting world of Sunday Soul Sante's 'Bohemian Rhapsody' held on 22nd January. Marvel at the fantastic dream-catchers, lush floral decor, rustic trinkets, and vibrant beads.

Enjoy the flea market, groove to the music, savour delectable food, and have loads of fun with friends, family, and fur babies. This special event offered a treasure trove of boho culture, featuring quirky home decor and trendy accessories for your free-spirited soul.

Sunday Soul Sante was a unique market where meticulous curation ensured the finest and most exclusive handmade merchandise. With over 250+ pop-up stalls celebrating creativity and Bohemian style, it provided a platform for talented artists from all over the country. Beyond being a shopper's paradise, it was a haven for foodies, pets, and music lovers alike.

The event also showcased the iconic rock band Indus Creed, a pioneer of rock music in India, who delivered an electrifying live performance and put India on the international rock map. An opening act by the Ananth Menon Quartet added to the excitement, presenting a mix of tracks from Woodstock '69 and other rock favourites.
