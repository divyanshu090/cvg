Pigtails & Ponys - Hair Accessories Brand

By Harish In Artists Posted October 17, 2016

Pigtails and Ponys, a Happy hair accessories brand, provides you with unique handmade hair accessories. Each piece, being hand done, has its own charm and grace. Our products are designed and crafted to share our natural love for picking up the simplest of things and transforming them into something beautiful.

Accessories being our first love, we studied together in Accessory design Department. As our friendship grew, becoming business partners did not seem a task as we were pretty well versed with each others strengths, weaknesses and work ethics. We used to make hair accessories for ourselves and got pretty good at it. Selling them was just a brainwave that we had one fine day. Being college students, Facebook was our best tool to market and sell, as it didn’t need any capital investment or intensive work. To back us up with a brand name, we came up with the name of ‘Pigtails and Ponys’ and its adorable pig logo which was fun, playful, unique and quirky. All the qualities we and our designs could relate to.

The Soul Sante Experience – Our first Sunday Soul Sante took us by storm. The response was phenomenal and was definitely a morale boost and the Sante was when we realised the true potential behind Pigtails and Ponys. The people, fellow vendors and the organizers together make the Santes an unforgettable and unique experience and memories we’ll always cherish.
